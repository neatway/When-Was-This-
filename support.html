Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

  • View a historical photo
  • Use the slider to guess what year it was taken
  • Tap "Lock In" to submit your guess
  • Swipe up to move to the next photo
  • Swipe down to review previous photos

How is scoring calculated?

  • Points are awarded based on how close your guess is to the actual year
  • Build up a streak by getting multiple photos correct
  • Higher streaks give you a score multiplier

Is my data collected?

No. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. Your scores are saved locally on your device only.

Where do the photos come from?

All historical photos are sourced from Wikimedia Commons and are in the public domain or used under Creative Commons licenses.

The app crashed or won't load

  • Try force closing the app and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • If the problem persists, contact us at the email above
