Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Introduction

When Was This? ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

When Was This? does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Photos or camera access
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is your game progress, including:

  • Your current score
  • Your streak count
  • History of photos you've guessed

This data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to any server or third party. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

When Was This? does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline and does not connect to the internet.

Photo Content

All historical photos included in the app are sourced from Wikimedia Commons and are either in the public domain or used under Creative Commons licenses. No user-generated content is collected or stored.

Children's Privacy

When Was This? does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our servers. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

International Users

When Was This? can be used anywhere in the world. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Data Security

While we don't collect data, the game progress stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features (encryption, passcode, etc.).

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. Since When Was This? does not collect any personal information, these rights are not applicable.

GDPR Compliance

When Was This? is compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as we do not collect, process, or store any personal data of users in the European Union or anywhere else.
